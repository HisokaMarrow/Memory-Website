The 3 Pillars of a Powerful Memory

These three techniques fuel each other — working together in a continuous loop to supercharge your memory.

Imagination

Create vivid, strange, and sensory-rich images. Use color, movement, sound, smell — the weirder the better.

Attention

Focus like your life depends on it. Imagine being paid a million dollars to remember. Your brain rises to the challenge.

Association

Link each item to the next with imagination. Example: A cloud playing Beethoven on a piano raining music notes.
