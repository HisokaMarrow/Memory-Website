Memorize key anchor dates ("Doomsdays") for each year and use mnemonics for tricky months. For example, remember "March 0" as the day before March 1 (Feb 28 or 29) ‚Äì it always falls on the same weekday as that year's Doomsday.

Example Calculation

Example: Find the weekday for July 4, 2025.
The anchor Doomsday for 2025 is Friday. July's Doomsday (7/11) falls on a Saturday that year. July 4 is 7 days before July 11, so it lands on the same weekday as the previous Saturday ‚Äì yielding Friday as the answer.
