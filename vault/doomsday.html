Doomsday Calculation Method

Master the technique to instantly find the day of the week for any date — a powerful memory tool and mental math exercise.

Step 1: Memorize Anchor Days

Learn the 'Doomsday' for each month — fixed dates like 4/4, 6/6, 8/8 that always fall on the same weekday each year.

Step 2: Calculate Year Code

Use simple formulas to find a 'year code' based on the last two digits of the year. This helps adjust for leap years and century shifts.

Step 3: Find the Final Day

Combine the anchor day and the year code adjustment to quickly calculate the day of the week for any given date.
